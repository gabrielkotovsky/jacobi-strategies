**annualised_volatility**
METHOD: standard deviation of returns per period --> annualize
no additional inputs

**sharpe_ratio**
METHOD: (annualized_return - risk_free_rate) / annualized_volatility
ADDITIONAL INPUTS: per period risk free rate
VALIDATION:

**tracking_error**
METHOD: std_dev(portfolio_return - benchmark_return)
ADDITIONAL INPUTS: per period benchmark returns, per period benchmark weights
VALIDATION:

**downside_deviation**
METHOD: sqrt(Σ(min(return - risk_free_rate, 0)²) / n)
ADDITIONAL INPUTS: per period risk free rate
VALIDATION:

**value_at_risk**
